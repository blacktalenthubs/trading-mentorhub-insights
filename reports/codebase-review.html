2. The Alert Pipeline (Heart of the System)

poll_cycle() [every 3 min, market hours]
  → yfinance: fetch 5-min intraday bars + prior day OHLC
  → get_spy_context(): SPY regime/trend/RSI for gating
  → evaluate_rules(): 20+ mechanical rules across 4,863 lines
      MA bounce (20/50/100/200), support bounce, gap fill,
      opening range breakout, VWAP bounce, target/stop hits
  → Dedup: unique constraint (symbol, alert_type, session_date)
  → Cooldown: 60-min post-stop-out suppression
  → AI conviction filter (optional, Claude scores 0-100)
  → generate_narrative(): Claude writes 2-5 sentence thesis
  → record_alert() to DB
  → notify() → Telegram (with ACK buttons) + Email
  → Paper trader auto-entry if enabled (Alpaca bracket orders)

Alert Rules (intraday_rules.py — 4,863 lines, 283 tests)

BUY Rules (Entry Signals)

  • MA_BOUNCE_20 — Price wicks within 0.3% of 20-bar MA
  • MA_BOUNCE_50 — Price wicks within 0.5% of 50-bar MA
  • MA_BOUNCE_100/200 — Wider bounce (0.5%-0.8%)
  • PRIOR_DAY_LOW_BOUNCE — Reclaim of yesterday's low
  • INSIDE_DAY_BREAKOUT — Break above inside day high + volume
  • SUPPORT_BOUNCE — Hold at support + recovery
  • OPENING_RANGE_BREAKOUT — Break first-hour high/low
  • VWAP_BOUNCE — Wick to VWAP + recovery

SELL Rules (Exit Signals)

  • TARGET_1_HIT / TARGET_2_HIT — Profit target reached
  • STOP_LOSS_HIT / AUTO_STOP_OUT — Stop breach
  • RESISTANCE_PRIOR_HIGH — Rejection at prior day high
  • MA_RESISTANCE — Rejection at 20/50/100/200 MA

Scoring (0-100)

  • Support proximity (0-25)
  • MA position (0-25)
  • Volume confirmation (0-25)
  • Confluence bands (0-25)
  • ≥75 = A+ BUY   50-74 = WAIT   <50 = AVOID

Dedup, Cooldowns & ACK Flow

MechanismHow It Works
DedupUnique constraint on (symbol, alert_type, session_date)
Cooldown60-min post-stop-out suppression prevents revenge trading
ACK GateSELL signals suppressed unless user ACK'd the BUY first
Re-fireBUY alerts unblocked once cooldown expires
