# Feature Specification: Social Media Launch — Twitter, TikTok, Instagram

**Status**: Draft
**Created**: 2026-04-12
**Author**: Claude (speckit)
**Priority**: High — launches alongside public rollout

## Overview

Launch TradeCoPilot on social media as an **AI-driven trading education and intelligence platform**. Strategy is **product-first, founder-invisible**: no face reveals, no guru persona. Content showcases what the AI platform does — AI scans catching entries, chart replays of winning trades, pattern education, AI Coach reading charts. Goal: build organic audience and trust through transparency, education, and visual proof.

## Problem Statement

The platform is ready for public rollout but has zero social presence. Competitors (Trade Ideas, TradingView, finance influencers) dominate social with either paid ads or personality-driven content. TradeCoPilot's unique edge is **AI that speaks for itself** — transparent signals, auditable track record, visual replays. This lends itself to a product-led, faceless content strategy that scales without a personal brand.

**Why faceless?** Founder does not want to be the product. The AI is the product. This also future-proofs: hiring or co-founding doesn't disrupt the brand.

## Goals

1. Establish consistent brand presence on Twitter/X and TikTok (launch platforms) and Instagram (follow-up)
2. Drive free trial signups via product demonstrations
3. Build a library of shareable content (AI scan clips, trade replays, pattern lessons)
4. Position TradeCoPilot as the "AI trading platform" in search and social feeds
5. Generate trust through transparent win/loss sharing

## Brand Positioning

### Brand Voice
- **Confident, not cocky** — "AI caught this setup" not "We called it"
- **Honest about losses** — share stops that hit with the same detail as wins
- **Educational first** — explain the pattern, not just the outcome
- **Data-driven** — every claim has a number (win rate, volume, VWAP distance)
- **No hype** — no rocket emojis, no moon, no "to the moon" language
- **Terminal aesthetic** — green on black, mono font, data-dense visuals

### What We Don't Do
- No founder face or voice-overs (faceless strategy)
- No "guru" persona — no "I called it" language
- No profit screenshots without context (entry, exit, reasoning)
- No cherry-picking — losses shown with wins
- No fear-mongering ("you're missing out")
- No financial advice — always frame as educational analysis

### Visual Identity
- **Colors**: Dark background (#0a0b0d), bullish green (#22c55e), bearish red (#ef4444), accent blue (#3b82f6), amber for WAIT signals
- **Typography**: Bricolage Grotesque (display), JetBrains Mono (data/prices)
- **Logo**: TradeCoPilot wordmark + crosshair icon
- **Chart aesthetic**: Clean candlesticks, annotated entry/stop/target lines
- **Tag**: "AI finds the trade. You decide."

## Content Pillars (5 Types)

### Pillar 1: Live AI Signals (Product Demo)
**Frequency**: 2-3 per trading day
**Format**: Screenshot + short caption + outcome follow-up
**Example Twitter post**:
```
AI just fired a LONG on ETH-USD

Entry: $2,207.34
Stop: $2,195.22 (session low)
T1: $2,218.22
T2: $2,225.84
Conviction: MEDIUM

Setup: Session low hold + VWAP reclaim with 1.2x volume

Full plan + replay after outcome 🔽
```
**Follow-up (2-4 hrs later)**:
```
Outcome: T1 hit at $2,218.22 ✓
P&L: +0.49% in 3 hours
Replay: [link]

AI saw the higher low + volume uptick. Textbook session-low defense.
```

### Pillar 2: Trade Replays (Proof)
**Frequency**: 1 per day (if winning trade exists)
**Format**: 15-second screen recording of cinematic replay
**Caption template**:
```
Here's what the AI saw at 10:02 AM:
→ PDL tested at $2,195
→ Higher low on 5th bar
→ Volume 1.3x avg on bounce
→ LONG fired at $2,207

3 hours later: T1 hit.

Watch the replay above 👆
```
**Platform**: TikTok (main), Twitter (short clip), Instagram Reels

### Pillar 3: Pattern Education (Trust/SEO)
**Frequency**: 1 per day
**Format**: Carousel (IG), thread (Twitter), 60-sec video (TikTok)
**Topic rotation** (14 patterns × 4 angles = 56 weeks of content):
1. What the pattern IS
2. When it FAILS (important — builds trust)
3. Real example from platform (live win rate)
4. Common mistakes
**Tie to landing page**: Every education post links to `/learn/patterns/:id`

### Pillar 4: Transparency (Trust)
**Frequency**: 2 per week
**Format**: Data-dense visuals
**Examples**:
- Weekly win/loss breakdown: "Last week: 12 LONGs, 9 wins, 3 stops. 75% win rate."
- Losing trade post-mortem: "This stopped out. Here's what the AI missed."
- Pattern performance: "PDL bounce 90-day win rate: 81% (22W / 5L)"
- "AI said WAIT on 40% of alerts this week — here's why that's the feature"

### Pillar 5: AI Behavior Stories (Engagement)
**Frequency**: 1-2 per week
**Format**: Short narrative tweets/videos
**Examples**:
- "AI refused to send a LONG signal today. Here's why it was right."
- "AI sent a RESISTANCE alert while you were in the trade. It saved $43/share."
- "AI said HIGHER LOW confirmed — 2 hours later: +2.4%"
- "AI vs Rule Engine: 3 months of head-to-head results"

## Risk & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Compliance / "financial advice" accusations** | Every post includes "Educational analysis, not financial advice" disclaimer. Pattern Library framing. Never say "buy this." |
| **Bad run of trades makes platform look bad** | Commit to transparency — post losses with equal detail. "AI got this wrong. Here's why." Turns bad runs into trust content. |
| **Copycat accounts / scams impersonating** | Trademark handle consistency, verify accounts when eligible, public track record differentiates from fakes. |
| **Platform policy changes (TikTok ban, Twitter algorithm)** | Diversify across 3+ platforms. Own audience via email list (capture from landing page). |
| **Content fatigue without personal brand** | Lean into product variety — 14 patterns × 5 pillars = plenty of angles. Community content eventually fills gaps. |
| **Time cost of content production** | Automate: build share-card generator in product. Batch produce weekly. |

## Compliance & Disclaimers

Every social post must include or clearly reference:
- "Not financial advice"
- "Past performance does not guarantee future results"
- "Educational analysis"

For TikTok / Instagram reels: add as text overlay or caption.
