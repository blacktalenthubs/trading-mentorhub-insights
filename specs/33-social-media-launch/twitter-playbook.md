# Twitter Daily Playbook — 10 Posts, Faceless Hooks

**For**: @tradecopilot_ai
**Goal**: 10 posts/day, zero founder face, every post leverages platform output

Each post template takes 60-90 seconds to fill in. Most come straight from alerts, replays, or track record — the platform generates content for you.

## Daily Writing Workflow (30 min)

**Morning (8:15 AM, 10 min):**
- Check /admin → AI game plan
- Draft posts #1 (pre-market) and #2 (bell prep)
- Schedule them via Buffer/Typefully/native Twitter

**Midday (12:00 PM, 10 min):**
- Check Telegram for today's AI signals
- Screenshot best one → post #3 (first signal) if not done
- Draft #4 (contrarian) from any WAIT or skipped alert
- Schedule #5 (pattern) from rotation queue
- Schedule #6 (midday)

**Post-close (4:30 PM, 10 min):**
- Screenshot today's closed trades → post #8 (closing) and #9 (EOD)
- Pick best win → #10 (evening thread) with replay link

## Rotation Content — Build a Queue

Keep a Google Doc / Notion with 50+ ready-to-post items:

- **14 pattern explainers** (one per Pattern Library entry)
- **10 loss post-mortems** (anonymized past losses)
- **10 market regime primers** (trending vs choppy, SPY context)
- **10 AI behavior stories** (times AI surprised you)
- **10 comparison posts** (AI vs rules, AI vs chat rooms, AI vs intuition)

Even on a quiet market day, you can still post from this queue.

## Engagement Rules

- **Reply to every comment** within 2 hours during market hours
- **Quote-tweet fintwit charts** with "Here's what the AI sees..." (1-2/day max, don't spam)
- **DM every new follower** a welcome + landing page link (first 100 only, then automate via Twitter's native autoresponder)
- **Like every mention** even negative ones (algo rewards engagement)

## Things to Avoid

- ❌ Rocket 🚀 emojis, moon 🌕 talk, "hopium"
- ❌ "I called it" — position the AI, not you
- ❌ Cherry-picked wins without losses
- ❌ Financial advice language ("buy this") — always "AI identified"
- ❌ Posting the same hook 2 days in a row
- ❌ Crypto Discord spam style — we're analytical, not hype-y

## Compliance Boilerplate

Put in your bio (not in every tweet):
```
Educational. Not financial advice.
```

Add to any tweet that looks prescriptive:
```
Educational analysis — not a recommendation.
```

- **Engagement rate** — target: 3%+ (industry average is 1%)
