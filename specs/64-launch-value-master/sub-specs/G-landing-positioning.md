# Sub-spec G — Landing & Positioning Alignment (P3)

**Parent:** #64 Launch Value Master · **Pillar:** Conversion · **Priority:** P3

## Overview
Make the landing page tell the truth and the whole truth: lead with the three things we actually deliver — **the few names worth watching, the high-trust alerts that teach, and AI that does the chart-staring** — while keeping the transparency spine that already sets us apart. Every claim must map to a shipped capability.

## Problem (current state)
Positioning is **already right in tone** — education-first, transparent, "for the hours you're not at one," "not another inbox of buy/sell calls." Two gaps:
1. **Claims vs. delivery** — the landing promises education in the flow ("every signal links to a teachable pattern," "learn from transparent outcomes"), but until Sub-spec C ships, the alert itself doesn't teach. The page over-promises relative to the live experience.
2. **No discovery or AI hero** — the "find the next mover early" value (Sub-spec B) and the token-metered AI value (Sub-spec F) aren't pitched at all.

## Target state
A landing that mirrors the launched product: a discovery hero, an education-in-the-flow hero, an AI-does-the-staring hero, and the transparency spine — each backed by a real, shipped capability. Nothing claimed that isn't delivered.

## Scope
- **Re-anchor the hero** around the busy-professional promise: *"Find the few names that matter, get alerts that explain themselves, and let AI do the chart-staring — for the hours you're not at one."*
- **Three value heroes**, each tied to a shipped sub-spec:
  - **Discovery** (B): "The next mover, early — a short ranked list with the why."
  - **Education-in-the-flow** (C): "Every alert teaches — the reasoning, the grade, the lesson, the autopsy."
  - **AI** (F): "Spend tokens, not hours — analyze any chart, get a thesis, review your journal."
- **Keep the spine:** transparency ("real outcomes, not synthetic win-rates"), "you make the call," "no card required · educational purposes only."
- **Claim audit:** every sentence on the landing maps to a shipped capability; remove or soften anything not yet delivered.
- **Beginner glossary** surfaced publicly (ties to Sub-spec C) so the education claim is visible pre-signup.

## Acceptance criteria
- **G-1:** Every value claim on the landing maps to a shipped, demonstrable capability.
- **G-2:** Discovery, education-in-the-flow, and AI are each represented as heroes.
- **G-3:** The transparency spine (real outcomes, "you make the call," educational-purposes) is retained.
- **G-4:** No claim describes a capability that isn't live (no over-promise).

## Out of scope
- Pricing-page redesign and full GTM (see spec #63 gtm-v1).
- Brand/visual identity overhaul.

## Notes
Sequenced last (P3) on purpose: the landing should be updated **after** B/C/F ship so it describes reality. Until then, soften the education-in-flow claims to match the current experience to preserve trust.
